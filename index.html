Open Source Hardware Stack

Built on accessible, affordable, and powerful components. Replicable by anyone.

Raspberry Pi Pico 2W

Dual-core RP2040 processor with built-in Wi-Fi logic. The brain of VayuNode handling data acquisition and MQTT transmission.

  • Dual-core Arm Cortex M0+
  • 2.4GHz 802.11n Wireless

Bosch BME688

The first gas sensor with Artificial Intelligence. Measures gas, humidity, temperature, and barometric pressure.

  • AI-enabled Gas Analysis
  • Detects VOCs & Sulphur

NB-IoT Connectivity

Industrial-grade cellular connectivity for remote deployments where Wi-Fi isn't available.

  • Low Power Consumption
  • Deep Indoor Penetration
