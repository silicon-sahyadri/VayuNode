Why VayuNode?

Most air quality data is proprietary. We are building an open data layer where revenue from industrial deployments funds open-source R&D.

Breadboard to Deployment

If it doesn't work on the bench, it won't work in the monsoon. We emphasize documentation so a hobbyist can build what a professional deploys.

Scalable Architecture

From Pico 2W to Cloud. Lightweight MQTT orchestration designed to handle 1,000+ nodes across Kerala.

Open Source Hardware Stack
